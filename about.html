About Us

"Victory" The School To Create Talented & Skillful Minds

The School was started in the year 1991 to cater the educational needs of the rural people of KODUR and the surrounding villages. To provide such good education, this was first school in Kodur, started with English medium from LKG to 10th Std with merely 87 Students in 1991.

Our " Victory's " Mission and Vision

     The development of the society and self-reliance of an individual can only be possible with education by which character is formed, strength of mind is increased, the intellect is expanded and by which one can stand on one's own feet.

    Since the day of its inception, we have been trying to our levels best to bring all new technology to these rural students like implementing of innovative programmes such as IIT Foundation Course, Vedic Mathematics, Abacus etc to enable the students, face the new competitive challenges in higher studies. We try to provide scientific approach to the students by maintaining fully equipped science lab, LCD projector, Audio visual room, Computer lab and so on...

    This further resulted to make many of our students to become Doctors, Engineers, Software technocrats, Govt. employees in various departments.

Our " Victory's " Academic Progress

    Since the year of its inception the school has been getting excellent results in both 10th class and 7th class public examinations.

    A great achievement our school got is: In SSC 2014, SSC2015, SSC2016 our students received PRATIBHA PURASKAR by Honourable Chief minister.

    We feel, a little bit proud to announce that it has become a customary to our school to have admissions into the most prestigious institution in AP, the IIIT (triple IT) every year since its establishment.
